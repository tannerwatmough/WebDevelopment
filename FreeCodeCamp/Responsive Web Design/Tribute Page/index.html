Background Information

David was born in Isleworth, Middlesex in England on May 8, 1926. His father was a principal who fostered Jewish refugee girls from Nazi Germany during the Second World War.

David is most famous for his work on various BBC Documentaries such as Planet Earth, ZooQuest, and others.
